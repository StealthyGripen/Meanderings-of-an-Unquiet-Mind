meanderings of an unquiet mind

experimental poetry

The following is a composite of generative art, poetry and code. The experience will cover several different styles of short-form poetry, all centered around the theme of sleeplessness and insomnia.

Some of the pieces are interactive, while others unfold before your eyes. Take a moment, relax and experience the bounds of your screen.
