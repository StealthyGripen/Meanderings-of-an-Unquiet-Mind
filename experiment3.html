Nonet

a nine line poem with nine syllables
in the first line, decreasing by one until
only a single syllable is left in the last line

9 │ 8 │ 7 │ 6 │ 5 │ 4 │ 3 │ 2 │ 1
syllable pattern

swirling slowly, winding and twisting

memories from forgotten times

appear in the darkest hours

their unyielding clutches

searching for ways to

loosen the very

shackles that

bind such

thought
